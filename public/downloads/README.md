## Creating Your Partnership Brochure

Your brochure should include:

### 1. Cover Page
- Event logo and branding
- "Partnership Opportunities" title
- Year and contact information

### 2. About VGS
- Mission and vision
- Event history and achievements
- Audience demographics
- Past event statistics

### 3. Partnership Tiers & Categories
- Title Sponsor
- Presenting Sponsor
- Platinum/Gold/Silver Sponsors
- Media Partners
- Technology Partners
- And all other categories...

### 4. Benefits by Tier
For each tier, detail:
- Logo placement (website, banners, materials)
- Social media mentions
- Booth space and size
- Complimentary passes
- Speaking opportunities
- Exclusive access

### 5. Exposure Metrics
- Website traffic
- Social media reach
- Event attendance (expected and past)
- Media coverage
- Livestream viewers

### 6. Additional Opportunities
- Merchandise partnerships
- Food & beverage partnerships
- Technology partnerships
- Custom packages

### 7. Success Stories
- Testimonials from past sponsors
- Case studies
- Photos from previous events

### 8. Contact Information
- Partnership team contact details
- Email: partnerships@vgs.com
- Phone: +1 (234) 567-890
- Website and social media links

### 9. Timeline & Deadlines
- Partnership application deadline
- Event dates
- Key milestone dates

### 10. Terms & Conditions
- Payment terms
- Cancellation policy
- Rights and obligations

## Tools to Create PDF

You can use:
- **Canva** (easiest, templates available)
- **Adobe InDesign** (professional)
- **Microsoft PowerPoint** → Export as PDF
- **Google Slides** → Download as PDF
- **Figma** → Export as PDF

## Design Tips

- Use your brand colors (purple, blue, cyan from the website)
- Include high-quality images from past events
- Make it visually appealing with graphics and icons
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use your gaming theme (gaming-inspired design elements)
- Include QR codes for easy contact

## File Specifications

- **Format**: PDF
- **Recommended Size**: 2-5 MB (optimized for web download)
- **Orientation**: Portrait preferred
- **Pages**: 8-16 pages recommended
- **Resolution**: 150 DPI for web (balance between quality and file size)
